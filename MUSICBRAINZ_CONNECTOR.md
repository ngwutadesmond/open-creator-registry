# Future MusicBrainz connector

Phase 6 defines only a `not_implemented` MusicBrainz connector descriptor. There is no operational
MusicBrainz request, configuration, scheduled run, or claim of integration.

A later reviewed connector should map the stable MusicBrainz artist ID, name, aliases, artist type,
country/area, public URL relationships, Wikidata relationship, and approved music-platform
relationships from JSON. It must use the source-required identifying User-Agent, follow current
rate and licence policies, keep checkpoints and provenance, and retain source responses only as
bounded checksums and allowlisted mapped fields. Implementation requires a separate phase and
current official-policy review.
