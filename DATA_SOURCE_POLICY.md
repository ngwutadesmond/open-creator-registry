# Data source policy

Phase 6 implements only a narrow, disabled-by-default Wikidata proof of concept. MusicBrainz has a
future contract but no operational connector. Reviewed administrator entry, bounded CSV/JSON
imports, and public submissions remain supported. Approved sources may supply external-profile
links, which retain licence, retrieval, connector, mapping, and source-entity provenance.

Open-source presence, a similar name, a source relationship, or a linked profile is evidence—not
identity proof, notoriety proof, endorsement, legal ownership, or proof of account control.
Automated ingestion creates pending candidates only. It never creates an approved creator, live
reservation, release, or platform availability decision.

Records remain subject to the project's correction and dispute workflow. Disputed, rejected,
private, or suppressed profile associations are excluded from public output. Source configuration
and ingestion administration are private and are never exposed by the public Worker.
